Play Frogger

Instructions

  • Move Frogger with W, A, S, D. (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • WATCH OUT FOR CARS! If you hit one, you lose a life!
  • When you lose all 3 lives, you lose the game!
  • YOU CAN'T SWIM. Stay out of the water, or you will lose a life.
  • Use the logs to get across the river, and land on a lillypad for safety to advance to the next level!
  • Your score will be increased by 100 each time you reach a lillypad.
  • The next level will be harder, with increased speed of cars and logs!
  • BEWARE OF THE ICY LOGS -- You'll slide into the river!
  • GOOD LUCK!
